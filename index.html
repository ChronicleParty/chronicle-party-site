The Story Behind the Story

Why I Built This

✦

I've always loved playing TTRPGs with my friends. And as a writer, I've always dreamed of doing something with those adventures — taking them from the table to the page, and turning them into a book worth reading. But I kept running into the same two walls.

The first: campaigns take years to complete. By the time you reach the end, you've forgotten some of the best moments. The second: even if I recorded every session, the sheer volume — hundreds of hours, full of banter and table-talk that never makes it into the story — made the project feel impossible to even start.

“These are stories that deserve to be shared and revisited, time and again.”

Then I became a dad. Twice over. And the window of time I had to write — or even to play — got a lot smaller. Sessions that used to happen weekly now happen months apart. The gaps between them are long enough to lose the thread entirely.

So I decided to build something. I used my own novel chapters and published short stories as the foundation and created an app that would take anyone's session recording and transform it into readable, engaging chapters — stripping away the noise, preserving the moments that matter, and giving you tools to shape how your story gets told. A way to catch up before next session. A way to memorialize the moments you never want to forget. A way to hand your kids the book of the adventure you went on with their uncle before they were born.

That's why I made Chronicle Party.

Ryan Standifird

Writer  ·  Father  ·  Dungeon Master  ·  Founder of Chronicle Party

The Process

From Recording to Chronicle

✦

Chronicle Party does the heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters — playing the game and reading the story after.

I
Upload Your Session

Drop in your audio or video recording. Chronicle Party handles transcription automatically, even across multiple files from a single session.

II
Identify Your Party

Tell the app who's who — map each voice to a character name, class, and pronouns. Add character backgrounds to deepen the prose.

III
Generate the Chronicle

The app splits your session into narrative beats and writes each one as a chapter of fiction — no banter, no table-talk, just the story.

IV
Shape Your Story

Edit, expand, trim, or rewrite any chapter. Use Find & Replace across your whole chronicle. Every word is yours to control.

Character Backgrounds Maintain a full party roster with background, personality, and item history that carries across sessions.
Chapter-by-Chapter Control Rewrite, expand, or trim any chapter with a single click. The story is yours — the AI is just the quill.
Session Finalization After each session, review AI-proposed background updates for your characters and apply the ones that fit your canon.
Prose, Not Transcript Chronicle Party writes fiction, not summaries. Your sessions become chapters you'll actually want to read — and share.
